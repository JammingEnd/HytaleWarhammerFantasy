## Overview

This mods aims to bring our beloved warhammer fantasy setting to hytale, mixing the iconic factions and troubles of Warhammer with Hytale's progression, biome's and combat. 

## Key Features
 * **Factions and Classes**
     * Empire, Skaven, Elves, Chaos, Dwarves, Kislev, Lizardman and many more
     * Each Faction has access to Faction specific classes (like Empire knight, Empire engineer, Empire Wizard), which provide unique traits that alter the way  you play
     * Visual interface for creating or updating your selected character
  
*  **Combat and abilities**
    * Expanded weaponry, faction specific (not exclusive) weapons with cool effects and abilities
    * Character Specific abilites and synergies (Skaven gain a combat gamble mechanic that has the chance to deal more damage but also damage you for example)

*  **Overhauled World**
    * More biomes that suit the different factions
    * Structures and NPC's that represent the different factions
